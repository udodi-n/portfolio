00. About Me

Hey, I’m Udodi — copywriter by day, web dev by night.

I’ve been writing since 2021. As a kid, I devoured cybercrime novels like Warcross by Marie Lu, The Blue Nowhere by Jeffery Deaver, and Zer0es by Chuck Wendig.

Over time, I realized writing is a unique strength. A way to shape how people think, feel, and act.

I’m endlessly curious about how small changes influence behavior. That curiosity led me into copywriting, consumer psychology, and even writing my own cybercrime novel (25% done so far).

Now, I help SaaS founders turn free users into paying customers using emails rooted in psychology and designed to convert.

My goal? Understand an entirety of human behavior... and leverage it to my advantage.
